Our Classes

Pre
Our Pre- K classes consist of children between the ages of two and three. The classroom setting is designed to provide our youngest children with a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment to grow and explore in. Toddlers will be given loving attention from our caring staff. The classroom is colourful and divided into carefully planned learning centres. It is filled with bright colours and a variety of materials for your child to manipulate, explore, snuggle, play with, and share. The room is especially designed to encourage your child's natural curiosity and desire to learn about her world.
K1
Our K1 class consists of our four year olds. Acquiring a strong sense of oneself is important for this age group. In K1, students focus on language arts, mathematics, developing fine and gross motor skills and self-help skills through various play based activities to help them become more aware of themselves and their peers.
K2
Our K2 class consists of our five year olds. This group masters the alphabet, sight and phonetic sound recognition, number recognition1-100, formal letter formation in writing our letters and sequential direction following. Our K2 class provides classroom activities that helps promotes skill development and discovery in an environment guided by biblical principles. Our five year olds are also prepared to make the transition from pre-school to higher education institutions.

Special Programme (Extended Day)

Our extended day special programme offers the same quality activities as our day programme with additional time to go more in depth with projects.

Classroom Overview

A fundamental key to integrally forming young children is the establishment of the proper learning environment. A core principle of this environment is the idea that, at this age, the most powerful motivator to learn is the natural joy that comes from learning.

Our classrooms are clearly laid out for students. The classrooms are equipped and prepared for the size and pace of preschool aged children and are designed to put them at ease by giving them freedom in an environment carefully prepared with attractive materials arranged on low shelves that can be reached by any child.

The tables and chairs are moveable so as to allow a variety of activities. All children are placed into classrooms based on developmental abilities and readiness and not just age.

Our classrooms are equipped with the latest smart board technologies as we seek to fully integrate technology into your child’s learning.

All classrooms enjoy music and movement, art and guided outdoor play.
